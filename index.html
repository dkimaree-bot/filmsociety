What we do

Whether you are interested in viewing films, networking with people, or learning more about filmmaking, The Seneca Film Society is “your space to explore cinema.”

  • Screenings & Movie Nights

    Watch films in a group setting and experience cinema the way it’s meant to be seen.

  • Creative Workshops

    Learn skills like editing, directing, screenwriting, or cinematography through beginner-friendly sessions.

  • Short Film Challenges

    Collaborate and create films with other students, even if you’re starting from zero.

  • Sound & Music Appreciation

    Explore how sound design and music shape emotion, and why it’s half the movie.

  • A Place to Belong

    Meet people who love film as much as you do, no gatekeeping, just vibes and community.

  • Opportunities & Networking

    Find events, collaborations, and experiences that help you grow your creative portfolio.
